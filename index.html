I’m Melody Chirinos and I'm a Business & Information Systems major with a minor in Data Analytics at New Jersey Institute of Technology

While at NJIT, I’ve had the chance to explore several different opportunities when it has come to my education.

Once I discovered that I wanted to work with data, I immediately made changes to my life to help me achieve this goal.

The ability to work in complex teams and explain complex concepts to others regardless of their experience is an amazing skill that I’ve come to sharpen.

I love learning new things and helping other achieve the best they can!
